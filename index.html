Mission

Logic as the substrate of trustworthy AI

GOFAI — Good Old-Fashioned Artificial Intelligence — was not a dead end. It was abandoned before its potential was understood. The Institute exists to ask what symbolic methods look like when they are designed for machine consumption rather than human readability, and what that makes possible for the trustworthiness of AI.

The work sits at the intersection of proof theory, formal logic, and the engineering of intelligent systems. Trustworthy AI (TAI), Responsible AI (RAI), and Explainable AI (XAI) are the context — but logic is not a compliance checkbox to bolt on at the end. It is the substrate. Our mission organises around three pillars.
