Support the Institute

An independent research body — sustained by partners who believe logic belongs at the foundation of trustworthy AI.

The Institute of GOFAI is an independent research body. We welcome research partnerships, joint grant applications, and philanthropic support from organisations and individuals who believe that rigorous symbolic methods have an essential part to play in building AI that is understandable, trustworthy, and genuinely useful.

Funding pays for research fellowships, a visiting programme, workshops, and the development of open tools for machine-oriented formal reasoning.
