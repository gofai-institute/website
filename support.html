Support the Institute

An independent research body — sustained by partners who believe logic belongs at the foundation of trustworthy AI.

The Institute of GOFAI is an independent research body. We welcome research partnerships, collaborative grants, and philanthropic support from organisations and individuals who believe that rigorous symbolic methods have an essential role in building AI that is interpretable, trustworthy, and genuinely useful.

Funding enables: research fellowships, a visiting programme, workshop organisation, and the development of open tools for machine-oriented formal reasoning.
